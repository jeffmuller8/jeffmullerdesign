## Overview

PFD designed for folks struggling with issues related to aging.

## Accessibility Features

- Easy to see and pull zipper loops
- Pouch for sunglasses
- Fleece lined hand warming pockets
- Comfort fit life jacket with soft foam
- Identification patch

The design prioritized usability for users with limited mobility or dexterity while maintaining all required safety standards.
