## Overview

Def Jam Records and Microsoft came to us to design a brand new experience for a Windows 8 presence.

## The Challenge

Design an app based on their current website that allowed for exploration of Def Jam Record members, fit into the Windows 8 ecosystem, and be as creative and fresh as the company itself.

## Solution

We reimagined an experience based on their site and offered a fun feature for users to pick their favorite artists as ingredients for criteria to explore new artists they may have never heard of.
