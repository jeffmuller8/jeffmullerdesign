About Me

I am a passionate designer hell bent on contributing my verse to the powerful play of design.

I have experience in both digital product and industrial design. I believe design is about solving the right problems. I rely on data driven strategies such as jobs-to-done, lean UX processes and hypothesis testing. I will not be testing fifty shades of blue to see which shade results in more clicks.

I don't subscribe to any design dogma. Instead, I practice Design Thinking through ideation, iteration and prototyping. I do this through observation, user interviews, journey mapping, brainstorming, storyboarding and user testing on prototypes.

Beyond Design
